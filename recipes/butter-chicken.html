Butter Chicken

Description

A recipe for butter chicken that my mum makes. It is the best way to make butter chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast pieces -- 2 pieces - cut into bite size pieces
  • Ginger garlic paste - 2tbsp
  • Tandoori Masala powder - 2tbsp
  • Onion -- 2 medium - cut into small pieces
  • Tomato -- 2 medium - cut into small pieces
  • Turmeric - 1/4 tsp
  • Chilli powder - 2 tsp (more if you want spicy)
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Cumin powder - 1 tsp
  • Yogurt or Cream - 1 Tbsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil - as needed

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with 1 Tbsp ginger garlic paste, salt and 1 Tbsp tandoori masala powder
  2. Once chicken is marinated, saute onions in about 1 -- 2 Tbsp oil until it turns golden brown
  3. Add the remaining 1 Tbsp ginger garlic paste, chopped tomato followed by turmeric, chilli powder, cumin powder and the remaining tandoori masala powder
  4. Saute on medium heat until the tomato cooks and becomes soft. After take the mixture and blend it in a blender
  5. In the same pan add a little oil add the marinated chicken and cook for a few minutes
  6. Add the blended mixture to the chicken and a little water (if needed)
  7. Cover and cook on medium heat until chicken is fully cooked
  8. Add 1 Tbsp Yogurt or cream before taking it off heat
